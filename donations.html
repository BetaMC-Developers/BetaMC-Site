Support the Server!

Donations are strictly used only to maintain the server! They are never required but are always welcome! Show your support for BMC, help keep us running and get some sweet perks! The server cost is currently approximately $50 CAD a month so any support is appreciated!

Donation ranks are manually issued, if possible please include your ingame name and discord name. Please open a ticket on the discord server to confirm your donation.

Donation Methods:

Paypal: paypal.me/betamcserver

Kofi: https://ko-fi.com/betamc

Crypto (ETH): 0x298aB2B5EF658660E52EAAea23c8FaCC79c0ae0B

Donator Rank ($15 CAD)

  • /nick
  • /hat
  • Coloured signs and chat messages
  • /lb t (see who built stuff)
  • Custom join and leave message (/cjm & /clm)
  • Access to all future perks!

The following donation ranks give little to no additional perks (they do show as a rank ingame however), they are for players who wish to contribute more to the server to assist with operating costs:

Donator+ ($30 CAD)

Donator++ ($60 CAD)

Donator+ and Donator++ can request for the colour of the "+" in their rank prefixes to be changed via opening a general ticket.

Donations stack on previous donations (Donator can donate $15 more and will become Donator+).

⚠️ Donations are not refundable. Including in the event you are banned from the server.
